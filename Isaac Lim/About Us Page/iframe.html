Our Roots

XL’s Cafe started off as just a small idea over late-night coffee chats. XL, the owner, always loved the aroma of freshly brewed beans, it reminded him of home when his mother used to brew him a hot cup of coffee for rainy and cozy days. He didn’t really plan to open a café at first... it just kind of happened. A few burnt batches and messy counters later, XL found the perfect blend both in coffee and in community. What began as a quiet dream turned into a place where people now laugh, work, and sip life one cup at a time
